The Visionaries

Deepika Padukone

Bio: I have acted in Indian movies for over 10 years and have done a handful of international box-office hits as well. I am based out of Mumbai, IN and early on in my career, I realized how fashion can drastically influence self-confidence and identity in a positive way. Since then, I have always loved experimenting with fashion on the red carpet and my involvement in Oasis became a no-brainer! P.S. I love dark chocolate!

Mikhail Varshavski

Bio: I am a fully certified family doctor (D.O.) based out of New Jersey. Fashion is a dear hobby of mine as I like to look good for my patients. And it just so happens that I live in New York, the fashion capital of the United States, which allows me to stay updated on fashion trends and subsequently experiment with Oasis Clothing. P.S. A fun fact about me: I have a 150 lb Chocolate Newfoundland named Bear!

Conception of Oasis

One mystical evening in December 2019, Mikhail Varshavski and Deepika Padukone met at a gala in Old Town, Scottsdale and bonded over their affinity to experiment with fashion. After talking for hours, they realized the strength of this common interest was far too valuable to ignore. And that same evening, Oasis Clothing was born.

The duo decided to commence their fashion experiment in Scottsdale as a way of paying homage to the gala that brought them together. Moreover, they resolved to utilize the Oasis brand as more than just tanother clothing boutique. They craved for Oasis to stimulate the local clothing industry and elevate provincial fashion brands by exclusively selling their attire. Fast forward to today, Oasis Clothing has sold over $2M worth of locally sourced clothing through their one and only Scottsdale, AZ location.
