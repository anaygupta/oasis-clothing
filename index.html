Oasis Clothing

Redefining clothing for young professionals

Located in the heart of Scottsdale, AZ, Oasis exclusively offers high-quality men's and women's clothing sourced from local designers while retaining a relaxed shopping experience.

Overview Section

MEN

For men, Oasis offers everything Everything from dress shirts and bow ties to plaid shirts and chino pants.

WOMEN

For women, Oasis offers everything Everything from formal blouses and stylish slacks to sundresses and sneakers.

COMING SOON

Over the next year, we plan to branch out and expand our clothing to include children, babies, and gifts.

The Beginning of Oasis

In 2019, Mikhail Varshavski and Deepika Padukone met at a gala in Scottsdale and bonded over their affinity to experiment with fashion. After talking for hours, they realized the strength of this common interest was too valuable to ignore. And that evening, Oasis was born.
